About Me

I'm a life-long learner that loves technology and solving problems.

Hello! My name is Kali and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My web development journey started with volunteering in a organization that taught programming to children using javascript to create an online game. As a problem solver, and natural tinker'er, I started learning more about programming. Finding ways to introduce computer science concepts to my 8th grade math class pushed me to learn more. Outside of my current position as a Data Center Operator, I consult and do freelance projects for clients.
